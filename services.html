How to Prepare for Your Session

To get the most out of your spiritual encounter, we recommend a few simple steps to center your energy and open your heart to the messages ahead.

Before the session, take 5 minutes to write down 1-3 specific questions or areas of your life you'd like focus on. Intentionality acts as a beacon for spiritual guidance.

Choose a quiet, comfortable location where you won't be interrupted. Light a candle or incense if it helps you feel grounded.

Drink plenty of water before and after session. Energetic work can be physically taxing, and water helps facilitate the flow of insight.
